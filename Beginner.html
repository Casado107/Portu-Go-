Beginner Level Course

In this course, we will cover the basics of the language, perfect for beginners who have no prior knowledge.

  • Introduction to the language
  • Alphabet and pronunciation
  • Basic vocabulary
  • Simple phrases and greetings
  • Grammar essentials
  • Common expressions and dialogues

By the end of the course, you will be able to introduce yourself, hold basic conversations, and understand simple texts in the language.
